About Us

This firm was started by Mrs. Chitra devarajan on 1992 expertise on all kinds of Design, Vasthu, liasoning and civil works. Later assisted by Mr.Naveen Devarajan, architect graduated from NIT-TRICHY, this firm specializes in out of the box designs, construction methods, exploration of materials and research. We are committed to creating environmentally conscious buildings that harmonize with their surroundings, promoting a sense of well-being and community. Our team of dedicated architects and designers work collaboratively with clients to bring their visions to life, ensuring each project is a unique reflection of their needs and aspirations.
